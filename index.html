Welcome to Lionsec & Mac Escorts.

We offer top-quality security services in Kolkata and in different parts of the country. We hold expertise in providing our clients with total security solutions like security guard services, sporting event security management and all kinds of event security management, secured cash transportation to banks and other financial institutions, fire control, bodyguards, facility management services etc. Our success lies in comprehending the client's specific requirements and meeting them consistently. Moreover, we guarantee a superior level of professionalism, discretion and confidentiality towards our clients. Our unyielding dedication to protecting our clients has earned their trust and respect.

We are equipped with 10 cash vans and 1 bullet resistant cash van. Our team of highly motivated and dedicated employees consists of more than 200 licensed gunman and more than 1000 well-trained security guards. We believe in providing quality and trained manpower to our clients for that we have kept a space of 9000 sq.ft exclusively for the training of our security guards in Hooghly district.

Lionsec & Mac Escorts possesses all licenses & registrations from governments and other statutory authorities for carrying out its operations in the country.
