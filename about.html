About us

Lionsec & Mac Escorts (P) Ltd was incorporated on 15th January 2007 by a team of professionals having very rich experience and credentials in security and manpower deployment service. The directors are of Professional background and also pioneers in the field of Personnel Management, Industrial relations and Safety & Security administrations. They have a combined experience of more than 70 man-years in providing such services to various industries.

MAC has a huge number of satisfied customers in various sectors ranging from Government Undertakings, renowned Private and Public sector Banks, Academic Institutions, Private Sector Companies, Trusts and Famous Tea Gardens in the country. MAC has been protecting Eden Gardens the pride of Kolkata for more than 20 years including various sports activities in the stadium. The company possesses all required Licences and Registrations from Govt. and other Statutory Authorities for providing Security and Man Power deployment services in Eastern and North-Eastern states.

Our Mission

To provide the most reliable, dependable and impeccable service to customers. To keep our customers and workforce always happy and contented. Always adhere to ethical business practices and transparent dealings with all concerned. To continuously review and improve our service to cope up with the new challenges of the industry.

Our Vision

To become an established brand name and 'customer's first choice' in the field of security, safety and man power deployment services.
